It's a Masta-piece

The Princess Bride

Two lovers journey to reunite in this storybook-tale. Amazing cast, amazing plot, perfect date-night movie.

Mad Max: Fury Road

A woman rebels against a tyrannical ruler in search for her homeland with the aid of a group of female prisoners, a psychotic worshiper and a drifter named Max. Everything I ever wanted out of an action drama.

The Incredibles

A superhero family in a world where supers are illegal. I have seen this movie so many times I can quote almost every scene.
