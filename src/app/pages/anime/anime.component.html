Below are some of my favorite anime picks delimited by some categories to help find what you are looking for. You can also see my complete list here.

Entry-Level Watches

For new anime watchers or convincing your friends that some anime aren't all trash.

One Punch Man

Some guy decides to become a superhero for fun and learns how to destroy enemies with - you guessed it - one punch. It's a solid comedy anime and satire of the superhero genre without forgetting why people like superheroes in the first place.

Mob Psycho 100

A middle schooler with awesome psychic power learns how to live a normal life. This anime has some of my favorite characters of all time (Reigen my beloved) and phenominal animation. Definitly something worth savoring.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Two brothers working as state alchemists search for a philosopher's stone after a failed attempt to save their mother. If you are looking for something that ascends past the genre of anime as a standalone work, then FMA:B is it. An absolute must-watch.

Feel-Good Anime

For when you need a break/something light to watch with a partner.

Yuri On Ice

It's hot men. They ice skate. I don't think I need to elaborate.

Ouran High School Host Club

Girl joins a new school and becomes a member of the "host club". Its a cheesy self-aware romance anime that anyone can enjoy (besides a couple scenes that don't age well).

Saiki K

A young psychic hides his powers from his friends in a desperate attempt to appear normal. Quick, snappy, and funny.

Nerd Picks

If you like searching "what did this mean?" after watching something then you might like these.

Serial Experiments Lain

Plays with the idea of internet culture before most of us were actually online. It is actually scary how much it got right.

Neon Genesis Evangelion

The pro-typical anime reccomendation. It's a mecha anime but really gets that noggin joggin by the end. Don't forget to watch the movie as well.

Hot Takes

You might think these are hot garbage, and they probably are! Either way, these are my soapbox nominations for secretly being good.

Black Lagoon

A typical japanese businessman gets involved with mercinaries in south-east Asia. Don't take this one too seriously and appreciate this testosterone overdosed anime for what it is.

Overlord

Some turbonerd gamer gets transported to a world where he can use his broken game stats to become an evil(?) overlord. Yeah it's an isekai but trust me on this one.

Hellsing Ultimate

A vampire killing vampires? A priest that weilds bayonetts as swords? An ex-cop that loves cannons? Its really silly and I love it.
